General Best Practices

⭐ Use BEE 5-star rated appliances — they consume 20–40% less electricity than lower-rated ones.
🌙 Run washing machines and dishwashers at night during off-peak hours to reduce grid load.
🌡 Set your AC to 24–26°C. Every 1°C lower increases energy consumption by about 6%.
🔌 Unplug chargers and electronics when not in use — standby power can account for 5–10% of your bill.
🪟 Use natural ventilation and ceiling fans before switching on the AC.
💡 Replace all incandescent and CFL bulbs with LED. They use 75% less energy and last 25x longer.
🧊 Keep the fridge at 3–5°C and don't place hot food directly inside. Clean the coils annually.
☀ Consider rooftop solar panels. In Andhra Pradesh, a 1 kWp system generates ~1400 units/year.
